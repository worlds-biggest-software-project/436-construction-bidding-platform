# Construction Bidding Platform

**Project ID:** 436
**Date:** 2026-05-02

## Overview

A construction bidding platform is a cloud-based system that connects general contractors (GCs), subcontractors, and suppliers during the preconstruction phase. It centralises bid package distribution, subcontractor invitation and outreach, document management, communication, and proposal assembly into a single workflow — replacing fragmented email, spreadsheet, and phone-based processes that still characterise much of the industry.

## Problem Statement

Preconstruction is one of the highest-risk phases in construction. GCs must identify qualified subcontractors, distribute large drawing sets and specifications, track invitation responses, manage addenda, and compile competitive bids — all under tight schedule pressure. Subcontractors receive invitation floods across incompatible systems and struggle to prioritise opportunities. Missing a subcontractor or distributing an outdated drawing revision can cause bid errors worth millions of dollars, or post-award disputes. The market has been slow to digitise relative to the broader construction industry.

## Core Features

- **Bid Package Management:** Creation and versioning of bid packages including drawings, specifications, and scope documents. Automatic distribution of addenda with confirmation tracking.
- **Subcontractor Outreach:** Searchable subcontractor databases with trade classification and geographic filtering. PlanHub connects GCs with hundreds of thousands of registered subcontractors and suppliers. Invitation tracking with bid/no-bid response capture.
- **Planroom:** Secure, controlled access to project documents for invited subcontractors. Version control ensures everyone works from current drawings.
- **Communication and RFI Management:** Centralised Q&A threads tied to specific bid packages, with responses distributed simultaneously to all bidders to maintain fairness.
- **Proposal Assembly:** Tools for GCs to compare received sub-bids, level scopes, and assemble the overall proposal. Procore supports the full lifecycle from bid creation through converting the winning proposal to a subcontract.
- **Bid Levelling:** Side-by-side comparison of subcontractor proposals with scope gap identification and normalisation tools.
- **Reporting and Analytics:** Pipeline dashboards, bid success rates by trade and geography, subcontractor response rate tracking.

## Market Landscape

PlanHub is a specialist cloud-based preconstruction bidding and planroom platform connecting GCs, subcontractors, and suppliers. SmartBid leads among mid-market commercial GCs for bid project data and subcontractor communications. Procore Bid Management serves the enterprise segment as part of its broader construction platform. BuildingConnected (Autodesk) is widely used by large GCs and integrates with Autodesk Construction Cloud. ConstructConnect offers project discovery alongside bid management. Archdesk and Bidi Contracting serve regional and specialty contractors.

## Key Differentiators

- Size and trade classification depth of the subcontractor network
- Simplicity of sub-contractor invitation and response workflow
- Integration with estimating software (Sage Estimating, ProEst, STACK)
- Ability to progress winning bids directly into contract and project management tools
- Mobile access for field-side document review during site visits

## Technical Considerations

- Large file handling for drawing sets (often hundreds of MB per project)
- Permissioned access control so each invited party sees only their scope
- Email-based invitation flows that work for subcontractors who won't log into a portal
- Integration with downstream project management platforms (Procore, Autodesk ACC, CMiC)
- Audit trail of all document distributions and communications for dispute resolution

## Monetisation

GC-side subscription (annual fee per seat or per active project), with subcontractor and supplier access often free to encourage network participation. Premium tiers for larger project counts, analytics, and estimating integrations. ConstructConnect monetises the project discovery / lead generation side additionally.
